## 冲刺 1: Gompertz 衰老死亡

**Component**: `GompertzMortality` — base_risk/current_risk/last_check_age_days/constitution/health_history

**函数**: `senescence_survival(age_pct, delta_pct, constitution, health_history) → f32`
- 公式: `h(t) = a × exp(b×t)`, `a = 50.0 × (1.5-constitution) × (1.0+health_history)`, `b = 6.0`
- ⚠️ 文档原始 `a = 0.001` 有误（与自身示例表差 5 个数量级），经用户确认改为 50.0
- `senescence_cause` 子模块: 4 种衰老死因 (OLD_AGE/ORGAN_FAILURE/SPIRIT_DECLINE/DISEASE)
- `sample_senescence_cause(age_pct, random)` — 按年龄加权抽样
- age_system 月度检查: age_pct >= 0.7 后每 30 天判定一次
- 死亡 → hp=0 + DeathCause(精确 senescence 码) → DeathWatchSystem 不覆盖
- 零年龄门禁 + 种族无关设计 (age_pct 归一化)
